Our Story

Founded in 1993, Dr. Ambedkar Convent School, Jammu is dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures young minds with knowledge, discipline, and compassion. Over more than three decades, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in Jammu education, guiding thousands of students from Pre-Nursery all the way to Class 12.

As a JKBOSE affiliated school, our approach blends strong academic fundamentals with character building, so that every child leaves us confident, capable and ready for the future.

Our Vision & Mission

At Dr. Ambedkar Convent School, we believe education is not just about academics — it is about shaping character, values and leadership.

We strive to provide a safe, inclusive and inspiring environment where students develop critical thinking, empathy and a lifelong passion for learning. Our mission is to empower every child to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society with compassion, creativity and confidence.
